Vertigo eSports

This was a website created for the eSports organization named Vertigo. Same concept as RebelLion, the goal was to display information and recruit for their team. I styled the site to match their dark colors and their sharp play style. The site was my second ever and took about two weeks to develop. This site is also fully responsive for all devices.
