Ice-Creamify

This was another creation for my second Code Day competition, I created it in about 3 hours as a side project when I wasn't working on our main project, Quizmatic. This site basically takes any word you type and puts it inside an ice cream cone. There are many identifiable flavors such as chocolate, mango, vanilla, and even mint chocolate chip. This website won best design at the competition.
