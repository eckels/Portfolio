Quizmatic

This was the project I primarily worked on at my second Code Day competition. I worked on it along side Robbie McCracken, Avi Saxena, Rahul Gudivada, Sean Kim, and Bhargav Yadavalli. We wanted to make a quiz like game for classrooms and create an educational and competitive game. We ended up winning the Best Overall award with our site and plan to publish it in the future once all the features are finished.
